Frequently asked Questions

We will answer any of your questions

How long does it take to fulfill my order?

Time to complete an order depends on three factors: the complexity of the painting, painting style and size. Usually it take 7 days excluding delivery time. In some cases, at the request of the customer, the period can be reduced to 5 days.

Note: If you decide to change something in the picture, make adjustments - this may take additional time, which is not taken into account in the agreed deadline.

Will I be able to see the painting before it is been shipped?

Yes. When it is ready, we will send you by e-mail a digital copy of your painting, so that you can, if necessary, make your corrections, if there are any. You can also see the painting "live" in our salon. Just contact us about it after making your order.

What format should be the photos?

We work with all known image formats! Through the site you can upload JPEG, TIFF, PNG, WEBP up to 50 megabytes in size. You must own the copyright or have permission to use the image. If you have a different image format (RAW, PDF), then you can use FTP uploader. If you want to take a photo from a paper photo (not digital), send it to us, we will scan, process and return the photo back to you along with your order!

Can I give special instructions to the painter

When you place your order on the site, you can add your comments in the dedicated text box area. You can also: combine objects from different photos into one or remove objects from a photo, you can change the background, change outfits, make a color painting from a black and white photo, as well as order a painting from a damaged or old photo.
